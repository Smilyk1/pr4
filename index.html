Borderlands 2 is an open-world third-person sci-fi shooter set on the planet Pandora. In the game, the player can take on the role of one of four heroes, each with their own unique abilities and characteristics. The player's task is to fight against enemy forces and complete various tasks to get to the mysterious treasure hidden deep in the heart of Pandora. The game offers a large selection of weapons, many enemies and dangers, as well as a lot of humor and plenty of action.
